About Me

I'm a self-taught front-end developer and web designer based in Okayama, Japan. My journey in tech is intertwined with my passion for education and entrepreneurship.

My Journey

My tech journey began with self-taught coding, which quickly evolved into a passion for creating impactful digital experiences. Alongside my business partner, I founded an English learning school here in Okayama, Japan.

As we grew our business, I honed my technical skills, focusing on front-end development and web design. Our efforts paid off as we saw our school's website climb the Google search rankings, reaching more students than ever before.

My role expanded beyond teaching as I developed custom tools and web applications to streamline our operations and enhance the learning experience for our students.
